A protocol fee is deducted from every bet's `amount` at placement. The fee rate depends on the operator signing the [`placement`](/contracts/pam/place) transaction.

## Fee schedule

| Operator | Fee |
|---|---|
| SoftSol Predict | 25 bps (0.25%) |
| Third-party | 10 bps (0.10%) |

## How fees work

Fees are handled transparently — users do not experience any reduction in their bet amount or payout potential. The escrow is funded as follows:

```
escrow = amount + max_win
```

The protocol fee is taken from within `amount` and routed to the yield vault for stakers. This happens in the background and does not affect the user-facing bet size or potential winnings. Stakers collect accumulated rewards at any time via [`claim`](/contracts/pam/claim) or when they [`unstake`](/contracts/pam/unstake).
