---
title: "Receive entropy"
description: "Pull Pyth randomness into a pending bet once the Fortuna keeper has fulfilled the request."
---

`receive_entropy` is called after [`place`](/contracts/pam/place) with `verifiable = true`. Once the Pyth Fortuna keeper reveals its randomness, anyone — the keeper, the operator, or a relayer — can call this instruction to write the `vrf_seed` into the bet.

Settlement is blocked until `receive_entropy` has run successfully.

## When to call

After `place`, poll or subscribe to the Pyth Entropy `entropy_request` account. When `is_fulfilled` (byte offset 177) becomes `true`, the request is ready. Call `receive_entropy` with the bet account and the request account.

The instruction verifies that:

- The Pyth keeper has fulfilled the request.
- The request's sequence number and provider match what was stored at placement.
- The requester recorded in the Pyth request matches the bet's escrow key.

## Accounts

| Account | Writable | Description |
|---|:---:|---|
| `bet` | ✓ | The `Bet` PDA to update with the revealed `vrf_seed`. |
| `entropy_request` | | The Pyth Entropy request account. Must be owned by the Pyth Entropy program and match the sequence and provider stored on the bet. |

## Errors

| Error | Cause |
|---|---|
| `EntropyNotReady` | The Pyth keeper has not yet fulfilled the request, or `receive_entropy` has already run for this bet. |
| `Unauthorized` | The request's sequence number, provider, or requester does not match what was recorded at placement. |
