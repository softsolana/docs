---
title: "Overview"
description: "Stake tokens to earn a share of the protocol fees generated by every bet."
---

The staking instructions let users deposit tokens into the protocol and earn USDC yield from the fee collected on every placed bet. Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed at any time without unstaking.

- [`stake`](/contracts/pam/stake) — deposit tokens to start earning yield.
- [`claim`](/contracts/pam/claim) — withdraw accumulated USDC rewards without touching your staked balance.
- [`unstake`](/contracts/pam/unstake) — withdraw staked tokens and claim all accumulated rewards in one transaction.
