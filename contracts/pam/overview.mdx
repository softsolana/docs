`softsol_pam` is an Anchor program on Solana. It handles two user-facing flows: **betting** and **staking**.

**Betting** — place bets with a configurable payout matrix and resolver set. Outcomes are attested off-chain by resolver keys and verified on-chain via Ed25519 signatures. Supports both observed outcomes (sports, prediction markets) and provably random outcomes via Pyth Entropy.

**Staking** — deposit tokens to earn a pro-rata share of the protocol fees generated by every bet.

## Betting flow

```
place → receive_entropy (verifiable only) → settle
```

1. [`place`](/contracts/pam/place) — the user deposits USDC and defines the bet: outcome slots, payout matrix, and the resolver set that will attest the result.
2. [`receive_entropy`](/contracts/pam/receive-entropy) — _(verifiable bets only)_ pulls Pyth Fortuna randomness into the bet once the keeper has fulfilled the request. Required before settlement.
3. [`settle`](/contracts/pam/settle) — resolvers submit their signed attestations as Ed25519 pre-instructions. The contract verifies the threshold is met and pays out according to the payout matrix.

## Staking flow

```
stake → claim → unstake
```

1. [`stake`](/contracts/pam/stake) — deposit tokens to start earning USDC yield from protocol fees.
2. [`claim`](/contracts/pam/claim) — withdraw accumulated USDC rewards without touching your stake.
3. [`unstake`](/contracts/pam/unstake) — withdraw staked tokens and claim all accumulated rewards in one transaction.

## Fees

A protocol fee is deducted from each bet's `amount` at placement. The fee is immediately routed to the yield vault and distributed to stakers pro-rata based on their share of total stake.
