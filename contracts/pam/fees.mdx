---
title: "Fees"
description: "Protocol fees collected at bet placement and distributed to stakers."
---

A protocol fee is deducted from every bet's `amount` at placement. The fee rate depends on the operator signing the transaction.

## Fee schedule

| Operator | Fee |
|---|---|
| SoftSol Predict | 25 bps (0.25%) |
| Third-party integration | 10 bps (0.10%) |

## How fees work

The fee is deducted from `amount` before the escrow is funded. The remainder — `net_stake` — is the base used for all payout calculations.

```
net_stake = amount - fee
escrow    = net_stake + max_win
```

The fee is transferred immediately to the yield vault and distributed to stakers pro-rata based on their share of total stake. Stakers can collect accumulated rewards at any time via [`claim`](/contracts/pam/claim) or when they [`unstake`](/contracts/pam/unstake).
