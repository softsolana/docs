---
title: "Overview"
description: "Instructions for managing player accounts, placing bets, and settling outcomes on the PAM program."
---

The Player Account Management instructions handle the full lifecycle of a bet — from platform setup and bet placement through to outcome resolution and settlement.

- [`initialize_platform`](/contracts/pam/initialize-platform) — set up a platform treasury before accepting bets.
- [`place`](/contracts/pam/place) — a player deposits USDC and defines their bet: outcome slots, payout matrix, and resolver set.
- [`receive_entropy`](/contracts/pam/receive-entropy) — for verifiable random bets, pulls Pyth Fortuna randomness into the bet once the keeper has fulfilled the request.
- [`settle`](/contracts/pam/settle) — resolvers submit signed attestations and the contract pays out based on the payout matrix.
