Predict lets you launch outcome markets on any event — sports, politics, finance, or anything else. Operators publish markets, set their margin, and let users trade positions using USDC. Pricing is handled automatically on-chain, meaning there is no need to manage liquidity or maintain an order book.

## How it works

Each market has a set of outcomes. Users buy shares in the outcome they believe will occur. Share prices reflect the market's implied probability of each outcome. If the outcome a user holds wins, their shares redeem at the winning payout rate.

## Flow

1. [`publish_market`](/contracts/pricing/publish-market) — an operator creates an outcome market with named outcomes and a margin rate.
2. [`get_quote`](/contracts/pricing/get-quote) — simulate a buy to preview shares, payout, and probability impact before submitting.
3. [`swap`](/contracts/pricing/swap) — buy outcome shares with USDC or sell shares back for USDC.
