Softsol is an on-chain B2B betting infrastructure provider built on a foundation of flexibility. Designed to be indistinguishable from traditional solutions, Softsol supports both Web2 and Web3 platforms — delivering the benefits of decentralisation without the friction. At the core of the ecosystem sits the PAM (Player Account Management) contract, serving as the primary gateway into the protocol.
